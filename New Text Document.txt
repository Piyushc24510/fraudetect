Online Review Fraud Detection System Using NLP
(Easy Explanation – BCA Level)
🔹 Problem Statement (Easy)

Aaj online shopping, hotels aur apps me log reviews padh kar decision lete hain.
Lekin kuch log fake reviews likhte hain taaki product ko zyada acha ya bura dikhaya ja sake.
In fake reviews ki wajah se customers confuse ho jaate hain.

👉 Is problem ko solve karne ke liye hum NLP based system banate hain jo batata hai:
review fake hai ya genuine.

🔹 Objective (Easy Points)

Fake reviews identify karna

NLP ka use karke review text analyze karna

User ko quick result dena

Online trust improve karna

🔹 NLP Kya Hai? (Very Simple)

NLP (Natural Language Processing) computer ko human language samjhane ki technique hai.

Example:

“This product is best ever!!!”
NLP is sentence ke words aur pattern analyze karta hai.

🔹 System Kaise Work Karta Hai?

1️⃣ User review enter karta hai
2️⃣ NLP review ko clean karta hai (extra words hata deta hai)
3️⃣ Machine Learning model check karta hai
4️⃣ Result show hota hai:

✅ Genuine Review

🚨 Fake Review

User Interface
